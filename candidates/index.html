CONTEXT PROPOSALS ORGANIZING CANDIDATES CONNECT

We want to thank everyone

who signed, gathered signatures, and otherwise helped out in our nominating petition drive.

We both collected above the 100 signatures minimum needed to qualify, but enough were disqualified so that neither of us were certified as candidates.

We’re going to continue to raise our issues by asking all Candidates to comment on our Proposals, making endorsements for the December 3rd elections.

The feedback and encouragement we’ve gotten from virtually everyone who responded has been amazing! We come out of this effort with a great mailing list, website, and a clear understanding of what is needed to empower indie musicians within and through the Union.

In solidarity,

M Ribot and David Stern

for the Indie Musicians Caucus
