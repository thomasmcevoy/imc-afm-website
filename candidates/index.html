Marc Ribot

In addition to fronting his own bands, Marc Ribot has recorded with dozens of major label, and several hundred independent label artists, and toured internationally since 1979. As steering committee member of the Noise Action coalition and Winter Jazz organizing committees, he participated in successful organizing drives at the Winter and Knitting Factory Jazz Festivals, and Knitting Factory records. He also co-founded the Artist Rights Caucus of Local 802, the Content Creators Coalition, and currently serves on the steering committee of the Music Workers Alliance. He joined the AFM in Lewiston Maine, 1976, has been a member of Local 802 since 1979, and served on the Board of RMA NY for several years.
