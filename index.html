CONTEXT PROPOSALS ORGANIZING CANDIDATES CONNECT

The Indie Musicians' Caucus is a coalition of independent artists who want to make the Union work for all of us.

As indie musicians, we record on independent labels and work in the related network of live venues, festivals, and touring circuits—encompassing all genres of music.

For nearly half a century, the AFM has failed to organize our sector. We believe the reasons for this are structural; we’re seeking office to support the IMC-AFM proposals to make successful organizing possible.

We're running two candidates, Marc Ribot and David Stern, for the office of Delegate to the 2022 AFM Convention in the December Local 802 elections, on behalf of the Indie Musicians Caucus of the AFM (IMC-AFM).

We need 100 signatures on Marc and David's nominating petitions to secure a spot on the ballot of this election. Please sign the petitions to show your support!
